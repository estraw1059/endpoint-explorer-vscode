# Save requests as templates

Dialed in a request you'll want again — a tricky payload, a bug repro, your standard smoke test? Hit **Save** in the request editor and name it.

Saved templates:

- appear in the **Saved Templates** view in the sidebar — click to reload the exact request
- survive re-running analysis (the Endpoints list gets overwritten; templates don't)
- live in `.endpoint-explorer/templates.json` in your workspace, so you can **commit them** and share with your team
